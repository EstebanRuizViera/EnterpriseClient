How do I create an App account?



To create an App account from the app:

  1. Download the app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Once the app is installed, tap image to open it.
  3. Tap Sign Up With Email or Phone Number (Android) or Create New Account (iPhone), then enter your email address or phone number (which will require a confirmation code) and tap Register
